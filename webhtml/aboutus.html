About Us

Diabetes, who cares? We care.

Glucope is a flagship product of Diabetes@Home, which helps you to manage your diabetes, recording data that can be monitored remotely by your clinician.

Our mission is to create a new digital way to protect your health with your own clinician more easily and conveniently.

Product features:

Record your blood glucose level, weight, doses of insulin taken, and exercise in Glucope according to the requirements of your clinician.

You can view each data tendency by checking the historical data recording and feel free to write any comments about the recorded data which your clinician will check frequently in case to ensure your health and give you some advice.

On the journey to health and wellness, we're right there with you.
